Chicken Kebab:

Ingredients: 1 pound (450 grams) boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces 1/2 cup plain yogurt 2 tablespoons lemon juice 2 tablespoons oil 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon paprika or red chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference) 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon garam masala 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder Salt to taste Skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes For garnish (optional): Chopped coriander leaves Lemon wedges Onion rings
Instructions: In a bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, oil, ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, paprika or red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix well to make a marinade. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator. For best results, marinate overnight. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, leaving a little gap between each piece. Place the skewers on the preheated grill or arrange them on a baking sheet if using the oven. Grill or bake the chicken kebabs for about 12-15 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are cooked through and nicely browned on all sides. Make sure the chicken is cooked thoroughly and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). Once cooked, remove the kebabs from the grill or oven and let them rest for a few minutes. Optional: Garnish the chicken kebabs with chopped coriander leaves and serve with lemon wedges and onion rings on the side. Serve the chicken kebabs hot as an appetizer or as a main course with naan bread, rice, or a salad. Enjoy your homemade chicken kebabs!
