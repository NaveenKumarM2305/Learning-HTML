Chicken Lollipop Recepie:


Chicken Lollipop:

Ingredients: 8-10 chicken wings 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon red chili sauce 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup 1 teaspoon vinegar 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce 1 teaspoon garam masala 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste) 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons cornstarch Oil for deep frying Spring onion greens (optional, for garnish)
Instructions: Start by preparing the chicken wings. Remove the skin from the wings, if desired. Cut the chicken wings at the joint to separate the drumettes and flats. Keep the wingtips intact as they will form the handle of the lollipops. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garam masala, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix well to form a marinade. Add the chicken wings to the marinade and coat them thoroughly. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes. For better flavor absorption, you can refrigerate them for a couple of hours or overnight. In a separate bowl, combine all-purpose flour and cornstarch. Mix well to make a coating mixture. Heat oil in a deep pan or skillet for deep frying. The oil should be hot but not smoking. Take each marinated chicken wing and roll the meat down towards the end to form a lollipop shape. Press the meat down to the bottom to create a tight lollipop shape. Dip each lollipop into the coating mixture, ensuring it is evenly coated. Shake off any excess coating. Carefully place the coated chicken lollipops into the hot oil. Fry them in batches for about 8-10 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Make sure the chicken is cooked through. Once cooked, remove the chicken lollipops from the oil and drain them on a paper towel to remove any excess oil. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens, if desired. Serve the chicken lollipops hot as an appetizer with your favorite dipping sauce or as a side dish. Enjoy the delicious and crispy chicken lollipops!
