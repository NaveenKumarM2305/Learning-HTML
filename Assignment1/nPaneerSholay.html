Paneer Sholay Kebab Recepie:


Paneer Sholay Kebab:

Ingredients: 200 grams paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan) 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon garam masala 1 teaspoon chaat masala 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder Salt to taste 2 tablespoons oil Skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes For the marinade: 1/4 cup thick yogurt 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon garam masala 1 teaspoon chaat masala 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder Salt to taste 1 tablespoon oil
Instructions: In a bowl, combine all the marinade ingredients and mix well. Add the paneer cubes to the marinade and gently coat them with the mixture. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes or refrigerate for a couple of hours for better flavor absorption. In a separate bowl, make a paste by combining ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, gram flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, chaat masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and oil. Mix well to form a smooth paste. Take the marinated paneer cubes and coat them with the prepared spice paste, ensuring each piece is well coated. Allow the paneer to rest for another 10-15 minutes. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Skewer the paneer cubes onto the soaked skewers, leaving a little gap between each piece. Grill the paneer skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are golden brown and slightly charred on all sides. Alternatively, you can also bake them in a preheated oven at 200°C (400°F) for about 15-20 minutes until cooked through. Once cooked, remove the paneer sholay kababs from the grill/oven and let them cool slightly. Serve the paneer sholay kababs hot with mint chutney, onion rings, and lemon wedges. Enjoy the delicious and spicy Paneer Sholay Kababs as a appetizer or as a part of your meal!
